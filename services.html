Services

What I do — and how it fits your operation.

I work on the unglamorous, high-impact layer of a business: the workflows, data pipelines, and manual processes that keep things running but shouldn't require this much effort.

🔁

Workflow Automation

Manual, repetitive processes are expensive — not just in labor hours, but in errors, delays, and the mental load they put on your team. I map your current workflow, identify what can be automated without adding complexity, and build reliable automated systems that run without babysitting.

  • Data entry and validation pipelines
  • Scheduled reporting and alert systems
  • Approval and review workflow automation
  • Document generation and distribution
  • Reconciliation and audit-trail processes
🔌

Legacy System Integration

Most businesses run on a combination of older systems and newer tools that don't talk to each other. The result is manual data transfers, duplicate entry, and reconciliation work. I connect your existing tools — even old or proprietary ones — so data flows where it needs to, automatically.

  • API and data bridge development
  • Automated exports from legacy platforms
  • Cross-system data synchronization
  • Flat-file and CSV pipeline automation
  • Incremental migration support
🗄️

Database & Spreadsheet Modernization

A spreadsheet that only one person can maintain is a liability — not an asset. I migrate fragile, undocumented Excel workbooks into structured, auditable data systems that your team can use and trust. The goal is always the simplest system that reliably does the job.

  • Workbook audit and risk assessment
  • Migration to structured databases (SQLite, PostgreSQL, etc.)
  • Formula and logic documentation
  • Role-appropriate access and data visibility
  • Training and handoff
🗺️

Operations Process Improvement

Sometimes the highest-leverage fix isn't technical — it's figuring out what the actual bottleneck is before building anything. I audit your current workflows, map how work actually moves through your team, and prioritize changes by business impact.

  • Current-state workflow mapping
  • Bottleneck and fragility identification
  • Change prioritization by ROI
  • Process documentation and SOPs
  • Recommendations with no obligation to implement
📋

Reporting & Dashboards

If you're spending meaningful time at the end of each week or month pulling numbers together by hand, that's a solvable problem. I build automated reporting pipelines that surface the right metrics to the right people, on a schedule, with no manual steps in between.

  • Automated KPI and metric reports
  • Scheduled delivery (email, Slack, shared folder)
  • Operational dashboards for internal teams
  • Client-facing summary reports
  • Data source consolidation
